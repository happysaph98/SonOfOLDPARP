FAQ

Here you will find a few answers to questions you might have.

  1. What does it mean by unsupported.

    2. Answer

    Basically, there will be no bug fixes or "Code Duct Tape" added, while we work on the new version of MSPARP. You can still regain lost mod by asking a Staff Member in the staffChat.
  2. When will the server be back up?

    3. Answer

    The thirteenth is our estimated date for the update, but it is a give or take situation, so the date is not set in stone. Please keep checking the MSPARP TUMBLR or MSPARP homepage for updates on the server status.
  3. I can't access my group chat. what can i do?

    4. Answer

    Try recreating your chat on Unsupported MSPARP This is a different server, so group chats will not exist until you re-create it here! If you continue having problems, ask a staff member by clicking here
  4. Will I be able to access my old logs?

    5. Answer

    Due to an unforeseen server issue, all logs from November, up to the date the MSPARP server went down, have been lost. Please forgive any inconveniences this may cause, but the loss was out of our control. All logs prior to November will become available when the real MSPARP server returns. Until then, they will not be accessible. Please save your logs while you are here on the unsupported server! Since it is a temporary server, the logs may be available to download when the main server returns. Also, try the solution suggested: here. It may help you recover some of your logs. If this does not work, we are sorry.
  5. It says that there are 50 people searching but I can't seem to connect to anyone. What could be wrong?

    6. Answer

    Matchmaker, the process that handles random connections between users, died. It needs to be revived. Contact MSPARP staff via: staffChat. This is the fastest way to let the administrators know it needs to be restarted. You can also try sending an Ask to this Tumblr: here, but it isn't checked frequently.
  6. I typed something but my post never appeared. What happened?

    7. Answer

    From time to time, when the server gets overloaded, posts get eaten and sometimes they just don't appear on your browser. Try refreshing your browser to see if that makes your post appear. If not, try holding down your control key, along with z and y. It may repost your last post. If none of these methods work, you may have to retype your post.
  7. HELP! I can't switch characters!! What could be wrong?

    8. Answer

    If you are experiencing difficulties switching characters, there could be a few potential reasons. (1) You may need to delete your cookies and/or clear out your cache.
    Note: that your cache is not the same as your browsing history.

  11. When will MSPARP get updated?

    12. Answer

    When the programmers have time to update it. Unfortunately, we are not sure when exactly this will be, as all people working on MSPARP are doing so on a voluntary basis and have homework/work/real lives that they need to deal with.
  12. I have a great idea! How can I let you know about it?

    13. Answer

    Submit it to the: MSPARP UserVoice so other users can vote on it. Be sure to check and see that someone else hasn't posted something similar. If someone has posted something similar, then you should vote on their suggestion so we know that lots of users are interested in that feature!
  13. I want to contribute to MSPARP. What can I do?

    14. Answer

    Try to be active on the: MSPARP Forums. The Forums are kinda slow and it would be great if we could get more people using them. You can post information about group chats, exchange advice on roleplaying, talk about Homestuck, post fan art, and do lots of other neat things there.
  14. I have encountered spammers in my chat and want to get rid of them. Can you help me?

    15. Answer

    Right now, there's not much we can do about the spammers. We recommend that you auto-silence new users and, when the options become available, hide system messages and block them. If you're really having issues with spammers, we suggest that you move your chat to Skype or Charat until MSPARP is more secure.
