BBCODES

Here you will find a few examples of BBCODES you might use.

  1. To make text italicized, enclose it in [i][/i].

    2. Example

    [i]Hello there![/i] will show up as Hello, world!
  2. To underline text, enclose it in [u][/u]

    3. Example

    [u]Having fun yet?[/u] will show up as Having fun yet?
  3. For strike through text, enclose your words in: [s][/s].

    4. Example

    [s]Goodbye![/s] will show up as Goodbye!
  4. To change the color of a block of text, enclose it in [color=#000000][/color], where #000000 can be replaced with whatever color hexcode you want.

    5. Example

    [color=#ff0000]So many colors![/color] becomes So many colors!
  5. For a spoiler, which is a bit of hidden font that becomes visible when a player clicks on the enclosing box, enclose your words in: [spoiler] [/spoiler] .

    6. Example

    [spoiler] Do you dare click this box? [/spoiler] will show up as a dotted box.
  6. To change your font, you simply put this in front of your font: [font=font name here] A list of font names can be found: here Be sure that the "font" part is lowercase.

    7. Example

    [font=Comic Sans Ms] Woah!, will show up as Woah!

  8. Text can have a different background color, too! Just enclose it in: [bgcolor=#000000][/bgcolor]. This works exactly the same as text color.

    9. Example

    [bgcolor=#68d192]This looks spiffy.[/bgcolor] becomes This looks spiffy.
  9. If you want a break in your text, simply put a [br] where you want the break to appear.

    10. Example

    This. [br] That., will appear as:
    This
    That.
  10. If you want a subscript text, just put: [sub] [/sub] around the words. Same for superscript: [sup] [/sup].

    11. Example

    [sub] Z [/sub] Z [sup] Z [/sup] will appear as: Z Z Z
  11. To make a shortened url, format it like this: [url=link here!] shortened name [/url].

    12. Example

    [url=msparp.com] MSPARP [/url] , Would show up as MSPARP.
